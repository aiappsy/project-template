Understanding Risk/Reward

  • 1:2 or higher is generally recommended
  • 1:1 or lower requires high win rate
  • Helps filter out low-probability trades
  • Improves profitability over the long term

Pro Tip

Aim for trades with at least 1:2 risk/reward ratio. This means your potential profit should be at least twice your potential loss.
