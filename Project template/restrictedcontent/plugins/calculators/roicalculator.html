Benefits of Trading Journals

  • Identify profitable and losing patterns
  • Optimize your trading strategy over time
  • Improve discipline and emotional control
  • Set realistic performance goals

Pro Tip

Review your trading journal weekly. Look for patterns in your winning and losing trades. What setups work best? What mistakes do you repeat? This regular review is key to continuous improvement.

Key Metrics to Track

Win Rate: % of profitable trades
Profit Factor: Gross profit / Gross loss
Expectancy: Avg profit per trade
ROI: Return on investment
