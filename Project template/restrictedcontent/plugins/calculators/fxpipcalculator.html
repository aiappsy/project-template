Understanding Pip Values

  • A pip is the smallest price move in forex trading
  • Pip value helps determine position risk
  • Essential for proper risk management
  • Varies by currency pair and lot size

Pro Tip

Always calculate pip values before entering a trade to ensure proper position sizing and risk management.
