The Power of Compounding

  • Compounding grows your money exponentially over time
  • Small, consistent contributions create significant wealth
  • Time is your greatest asset in compounding
  • Higher compounding frequency accelerates growth

Pro Tip

Start investing early! A 25-year-old investing $500/month at 8% annual return would have over $1.4 million by age 65.

Rule of 72

Divide 72 by your annual return rate to estimate how many years it takes to double your investment.
