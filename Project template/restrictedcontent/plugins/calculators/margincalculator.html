Understanding Margin Trading

  • Margin allows you to control larger positions with less capital
  • Higher leverage increases both potential profits and losses
  • Monitor margin levels to avoid margin calls
  • Proper margin management is crucial for long-term success

Pro Tip

Keep your margin usage below 50% to maintain a healthy buffer and avoid margin calls during market volatility.
